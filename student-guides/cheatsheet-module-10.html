Building an Enterprise AI Strategy

An AI strategy is not a technology plan — it's a business plan that uses AI as a lever. Start with business outcomes, not AI capabilities.

The AI Strategy Framework

Business Goals → Use Case Identification → Prioritization → Pilot → Scale → Optimize

Step 1: Identify High-Value Use Cases

Use Case Prioritization Matrix

Score each potential AI use case on two dimensions:

Criteria Weight Scoring (1–5)
Business Impact
Revenue potential 20% How much revenue can this generate or protect?
Cost savings 20% How much time/money does this save?
Strategic alignment 10% Does this support key business objectives?
Feasibility
Data readiness 15% Is quality data available?
Technical complexity 15% How hard is this to build/deploy?
Risk level 10% Regulatory, reputational, operational risk?
Time to value 10% How quickly can we see results?

Prioritize: High impact + High feasibility = Start here.

Step 2: Calculate AI ROI

ROI Formula

AI ROI = (Total Benefits – Total Costs) / Total Costs × 100%

Benefits to Quantify

Benefit Category How to Measure Example
Time Savings Hours saved × hourly cost 500 employees × 5 hrs/month × $50/hr = $125K/month
Error Reduction Error rate decrease × cost per error 30% fewer invoice errors × $200/error = $60K/year
Revenue Uplift Incremental revenue from AI-driven actions AI upsell recommendations: +$500K/year
Faster Decisions Time-to-decision reduction × opportunity value Contract review: 5 days → 1 day
Customer Satisfaction NPS/CSAT improvement → retention value +10 NPS points → 5% lower churn

Costs to Include

Cost Category Components
Licensing Per-seat or per-token fees, annual subscriptions
Implementation Integration, customization, consulting
Infrastructure Cloud compute, storage, vector databases
Training Employee onboarding, ongoing education
Maintenance Monitoring, updates, support
Governance Compliance, auditing, policy management

Sample ROI Calculation

Item Annual Cost/Benefit
ChatGPT Enterprise (200 users) –$144,000
Implementation & integration –$50,000
Training program –$20,000
Time savings (200 users × 8 hrs/mo × $50/hr) +$960,000
Error reduction (customer communications) +$80,000
Net Benefit +$826,000
ROI 386%

Step 3: Build the Roadmap

12-Month AI Roadmap Template

Phase Timeline Activities Deliverables
Discovery Months 1–2 Inventory current AI use, identify use cases, assess data readiness Use case backlog, data assessment report
Foundation Months 2–4 Select tools, establish governance, draft policies, train first cohort AI policy, approved tool list, trained champions
Pilot Months 4–6 Deploy 2–3 high-priority use cases with limited user group Pilot results, lessons learned, ROI estimates
Expand Months 6–9 Scale successful pilots, onboard additional teams, refine processes Expanded deployment, updated policies
Optimize Months 9–12 Measure ROI, optimize workflows, explore advanced use cases (RAG, agents) ROI report, optimization roadmap, year 2 plan

Common Pitfalls to Avoid

Pitfall Why It Fails What to Do Instead
Starting with technology "We need AI" without clear business need Start with business problems
Boiling the ocean Trying to AI-enable everything at once Pick 2–3 high-impact pilots
Ignoring change management Tools deployed but nobody uses them Invest in training and champions
No governance Uncontrolled risk accumulation Build governance alongside deployment
No measurement Can't prove value to leadership Define KPIs before launch
Vendor lock-in Dependency on single provider Multi-vendor strategy, data portability
