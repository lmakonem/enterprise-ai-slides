Success Metrics (KPIs)

  1. Adoption Rate: % of employees using approved AI tools weekly. (Target: >50%)
  2. Efficiency: Avg. hours saved per employee per week. (Target: >2 hours)
  3. Quality: Employee satisfaction score with AI outputs. (Target: >4/5)
  4. Risk: Number of reported security/policy incidents. (Target: 0)
