Category: Role-Playing & Simulation

Prompt 4: [Name] (e.g., "Interview Practice")

Goal: Simulate a job interview.
Tools: Claude, ChatGPT (Voice Mode)
Prompt Template:

Act as a hiring manager for a [Role] at a [Company Type].

Ask me 5 common interview questions, one at a time. After I answer each question, provide constructive feedback on my response before moving to the next one.

Start by asking the first question.

Example Output:
"Great! Let's begin. Tell me about a time you handled a difficult conflict at work."

Tips for Better Prompting

  1. Be Specific: Instead of "Write an article," say "Write a 500-word article about..."
  2. Give Context: "Act as a senior engineer" or "Explain like I'm 5."
  3. Use Examples: Providing 1-2 examples (Few-Shot) dramatically improves quality.
  4. Iterate: If the first result is bad, refine your prompt. "Make it shorter," "Add more data," etc.
