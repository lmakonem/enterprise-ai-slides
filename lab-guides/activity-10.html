Part 1: Opportunity Identification (15 min)

Think of a real problem in your work (or make one up).
* Example: "Customer Support is overwhelmed. They spend 4 hours/day answering the same 5 questions."
* Solution: AI Chatbot or Email Drafter.
* Goal: Reduce response time by 50%.

Part 2: ROI Calculation (20 min)

Open the ROI Calculator Template.
Fill in the numbers. Be conservative!

  1. Investment (Costs):

    • Software: $50/user/month x 10 users x 12 months = $6,000.
    • Dev/Setup: $5,000 (Consultant or internal hours).
    • Training: $2,000 (Workshops).
    • Total: $13,000.

  2. Returns (Benefits):

    • Time Saved: 2 hours/day x $30/hour x 5 days/week x 48 weeks x 10 users = $144,000/year value.
    • Revenue Increase: 5% more sales due to faster replies = $50,000.
    • Total: $194,000.

  3. Calculate:

    • ROI = ($194k - $13k) / $13k = 1,392% (Huge!).
    • Even if you are wrong by 50%, it's still a massive win.

Part 3: Building the Roadmap (30 min)

Open the AI Roadmap Template.
Plan out the next 3 months.

  • Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4):

    • "Get approval for budget."
    • "Select the tool (e.g., Intercom Fin, ChatGPT Team)."
    • "Train 2 'champion' users."

  • Phase 2 (Weeks 5-8):

    • "Pilot with 10% of tickets."
    • "Review accuracy weekly."
    • "Refine the prompts."

  • Phase 3 (Weeks 9-12):

    • "Roll out to full team."
    • "Measure KPIs (Response Time, CSAT)."
    • "Celebrate the win!"

Part 4: Risk Mitigation (15 min)

What could go wrong? Add these to your roadmap.
* Hallucination: "Bot gives wrong refund policy." -> Mitigation: "Human must approve all refunds > $50."
* Employee Fear: "Support agents worry about job loss." -> Mitigation: "Reframe as 'AI handles the boring stuff so you can handle the complex cases.'"

Part 5: Final Review (10 min)

Does your plan look realistic? Are the dates achievable?
Show it to a peer (or imagine pitching it to your boss).
What's the one question they will ask? (Usually: "Is our data safe?"). Make sure you have an answer.
