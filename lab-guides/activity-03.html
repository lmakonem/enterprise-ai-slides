Task 1: Summarize a Report (10 min)

Find a short news article or report (approx. 500 words). Paste it into each AI with the prompt:

"Summarize this in 3 bullet points for an executive."

  • Evaluate: Did they capture the main point? Were they concise?

Task 2: Draft an Email (10 min)

Prompt:

"Draft a polite email to a client explaining that their project is delayed by 2 weeks due to a supply chain issue. Offer a 5% discount on their next order as an apology."

  • Evaluate: Tone, professionalism, clarity.

Task 3: Create a Project Timeline (10 min)

Prompt:

"Create a 4-week project timeline for launching a new website. Include phases for Design, Development, Testing, and Launch. Output as a markdown table."

  • Evaluate: Logic, formatting, detail.

Task 4: Analyze Data (15 min)

Upload sales_data.csv (or paste the text if upload isn't available) into each AI.
Prompt:

"Analyze this sales data. Which product has the highest total revenue? Which region sold the most units?"

  • Evaluate: Accuracy (did they do the math right?), insight.

Task 5: Meeting Minutes (10 min)

Paste the meeting_transcript.txt.
Prompt:

"Generate meeting minutes from this transcript. Include Attendees, Decisions Made, and Action Items with owners."

  • Evaluate: Structure, accuracy (did they assign tasks correctly?).
