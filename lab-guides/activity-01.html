üìù Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Account Setup (5 min)

If you haven't already, sign up for free accounts on all three platforms.
1. ChatGPT: Click "Sign Up", use email/Google/Microsoft.
2. Claude: Click "Continue with Email" or Google. You may need to verify a phone number.
3. Gemini: Log in with your existing Google account.

Step 2: The "Control" Questions (15 min)

We need to ask the exact same questions to all three AIs to make a fair comparison. Open three tabs, one for each AI.

Copy and paste these prompts into each AI:

  1. The Strategy Question:
    > "Explain 'Generative AI' to a non-technical CEO in under 100 words. Focus on business value, not technology."

  2. The Creative Task:
    > "Write a polite but firm email declining a vendor's proposal because their price is too high. Suggest we revisit in Q4."

  3. The Analytical Task:
    > "I have a dataset of 10,000 customer reviews. 20% are negative. What are 3 specific ways I could use AI to analyze this data to improve customer retention?"

  4. The Logic Trap (Hallucination Check):
    > "Who was the CEO of OpenAI in 2010?" (Note: OpenAI was founded in late 2015. See if they catch the error.)

  5. The Formatting Task:
    > "Create a table comparing a Sole Proprietorship vs. LLC. Include columns for: Liability, Taxes, Cost to Start, and paperwork."

Step 3: Evaluate & Score (10 min)

Open the AI Tool Comparison Scorecard.

For each response, rate the AI on a scale of 1-5:
- Accuracy: Did it answer the prompt correctly? Did it catch the logic trap?
- Tone: Was the email professional? Was the CEO explanation accessible?
- Formatting: Did the table look good? Was the text easy to scan?

üß† Reflection Questions

  1. Which AI felt the most "human" in its writing style?
  2. Which one was the fastest?
  3. Did any of them fail the "Logic Trap" (Question 4)?
  4. If you had to pay for only one, which would you choose for your specific job role?
